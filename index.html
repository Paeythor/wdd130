Utah

Provo is a city in the state of Utah, located about 45 minutes south of Salt Lake City. It is known for being the beautiful home of Brigham Young University and is surrounded by scenic mountains. Provo has a strong tech scene, making it an ideal location for those pursuing careers in technology and innovation.

Utah is a state in the western United States. It is bordered by Colorado to the east, Wyoming to the northeast, Idaho to the north, Arizona to the south, and Nevada to the west. It also touches a corner of New Mexico in the southeast. The state is a center of transportation, education, information technology, and research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation.
