Our mission at Mountain Home Whitewater Rafting is to bring people together through thrilling, safe, and unforgettable rafting adventures in the heart of nature. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rafter, we have something exciting waiting for you.

History

Mountain Home Whitewater Rafting was founded in 1998 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts. Since then, we've guided thousands of guests down some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in the country.
